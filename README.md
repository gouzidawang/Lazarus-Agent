## 💸 The Problem: Web3's Cyber Graveyards
Every Web3 user’s wallet is a graveyard of fragmented, forgotten liquidity. 
We have:
- $2.30 of $ETH dust left after gas fees.
- $150 stuck in a deprecated DeFi protocol from 2024.
- Unclaimed airdrops we don't even know we qualify for.
- Assets scattered across 5 different Layer 2 networks.

Current portfolio trackers only tell you what you have. They don't help you clean up the mess. The cognitive load and gas costs of manually recovering these micro-assets make it a nightmare for retail and whales alike. **Billions of dollars in liquidity are sleeping.**

## ⚡ The Solution: Lazarus Agent
Lazarus is an intent-centric AI agent built on OKX Onchain OS. You simply tell the agent: *"Sweep my wallet and consolidate all my dust into USDC."*

The Agent will:
1. **Deep Scan (via OKX OS):** Crawl across all supported EVM chains to find hidden balances, unclaimed airdrops, and forgotten LP positions.
2. **Scam Filtering:** Automatically ignore honeypots and worthless zero-liquidity tokens.
3. **Profitability Routing:** Calculate gas costs. If it costs $5 in gas to recover $2 of dust, Lazarus skips it.
4. **Resurrection Payload:** Bundle the profitable recovery transactions into a single, signable payload.
